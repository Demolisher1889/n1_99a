-Who are Slaughter to Prevail?

Slaughter to Prevail is a Russian deathcore band that formed in 2013. The band consists of Alexey Lesukov (vocals), Jack Simmons (guitar) and Evgeny Novikov (drums). The members range in age from 21 to 25 years old. Their music is characterized by heavy guitars and aggressive vocals.

-The Music of Slaughter to Prevail

Slaughter to Prevail have released two full-length albums and an EP since their formation. Their debut album 'Misery Sermon' was released in 2017 and featured the single 'Shadow', which gained them a lot of attention for its heavy riffs and intense lyrics. Their follow-up album 'Chapters of Misery' was released in 2019 and featured guest appearances from Rob Maramonte and Spencer Charnas. The album was well-received by fans and critics alike, with many praising the band for their technical musicianship and lyrical content.

-Live Performances

Slaughter to Prevail have toured extensively across Europe and North America in support of their albums. They have played some of the biggest festivals in the metalcore scene such as Download Festival and Wacken Open Air. Their energetic live shows have been praised by fans for their intensity and energy.

-Legacy

Slaughter to Prevail have quickly become one of the most respected bands in the metalcore scene. Their unique blend of heavy riffs, intense vocals and technical musicianship has earned them a dedicated fan base all over the world. With two full-length albums under their belt and more touring plans on the horizon, Slaughter to Prevail are sure to continue making an impact on the metalcore scene for years to come.
